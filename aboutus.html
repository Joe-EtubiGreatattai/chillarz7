About Us

Welcome to Chillarz 7, a premier entertainment destination that takes leisure and hospitality to the next level. Founded by Mr. Thomas Opaluwa, Chillarz 7 has become synonymous with excellence, offering an unparalleled experience to our valued guests.

At Chillarz 7, we take pride in curating a diverse array of facilities and services under one roof. Our expansive establishment boasts a sophisticated restaurant, a vibrant bar, a chic lounge, a pulsating nightclub, and cozy lodging options. Whether you're a food enthusiast, party lover, or a traveler seeking comfort, we have something special for you.

Our Restaurant presents an enticing menu that caters to discerning palates. From our signature Barbecue Fish, delightful Pizzas, succulent Burgers, to an extensive selection of mouthwatering delicacies, our talented chefs create culinary wonders that will leave you craving for more.

In our Bar & Lounge, unwind and savor refreshing drinks amidst a relaxed ambiance. Our skilled mixologists craft delightful concoctions and pour premium spirits to elevate your mood and elevate your experience.

Our Nightclub is where the real magic happens. Get ready to dance the night away with top-notch DJs spinning electrifying tracks, dazzling light shows, and an energetic crowd. Chillarz 7 Nightclub is the place to be for those seeking unforgettable nightlife experiences.

For travelers and explorers, our Lodging offers a cozy and comfortable stay. Rest easy after a night of excitement at our club and indulge in our warm hospitality. Our well-appointed rooms and top-notch amenities ensure a relaxing and rejuvenating stay.

At Chillarz 7, our dedication to providing exceptional services and creating cherished memories sets us apart. We are driven by our passion for hospitality and strive to make every guest feel special. Our team of professionals ensures that your visit to Chillarz 7 is nothing short of remarkable.

Join us at Chillarz 7 and immerse yourself in an unforgettable experience. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a night out with friends, or simply seeking a moment of relaxation, we guarantee you'll leave with a smile on your face.
